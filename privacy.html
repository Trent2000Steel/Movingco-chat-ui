Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

MovingCo respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you choose to share with us, such as:

  • Your chat messages (to provide quotes and coordinate services)
  • Your move details, including origin, destination, timing, and item descriptions
  • Optional contact information if you provide it (email or phone)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate accurate moving quotes
  • Coordinate with third-party movers and carriers
  • Improve the quality and clarity of our AI chat experience

3. AI Disclosure

Our chat interface uses AI technology to gather move details and provide recommendations. Conversations may be reviewed to improve service quality. Do not submit sensitive personal or financial information via chat.

4. Data Sharing

We may share your move details with vetted third-party movers or carriers to coordinate your service. We do not sell or rent your information to marketers.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This site does not currently use cookies for tracking or advertising. If that changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. You share information with us at your own discretion.

7. Contact

To ask questions or request deletion of your data, email: support@movingco.ai.
